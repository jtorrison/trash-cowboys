— Got Questions? —

Straight-Shootin' Answers

How do I get a quote?

Easiest way: text a photo of your junk to (303) 555-0199. You'll have a quote back within an hour, usually much faster. You can also call, or fill out the form below.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes — same-day is available most days across Denver Metro. Call before noon for the best chance of a same-day slot. Same-day jobs are priced at a priority rate.

How does your pricing work?

We don't post flat rates because every job is different — volume, access, stairs, materials. You'll get a free, upfront quote before we touch a thing. No pricing games. You approve, we haul.

What areas do you serve?

All of Denver Metro and the Front Range — Aurora, Golden, Lakewood, Westminster, Boulder, Wheat Ridge, Applewood, Arvada, Broomfield, Littleton, Highlands Ranch, Parker, Castle Rock, and everywhere in between.

Are your estimates really free?

Yep. Photo quotes, phone quotes, and on-site estimates are all free. No deposit, no commitment until the crew arrives and you approve the final number.

What do you do with the junk?

Donate first, recycle second, landfill last. Usable items go to Denver-area charities. Metals, electronics, wood, concrete, mattresses, and yard waste get sorted at certified Colorado recycling facilities. Only true waste ends up in a landfill — about 40% of what we haul.

Do you haul electronics, mattresses, and hazardous items?

Yes to all three. Electronics go to certified e-waste facilities. Mattresses are separated and recycled. Hazardous items (paint, chemicals, oils) are taken to proper Colorado disposal sites — we don't just dump them.

Do you work with property managers and contractors?

Absolutely — commercial accounts are a big part of what we do. We offer priority scheduling, dedicated contacts, and recurring service contracts. Reach out and we'll set it up.
